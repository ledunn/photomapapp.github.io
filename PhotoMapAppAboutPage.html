    • About this project

    neighborhood.pictures was created as a way to help people interact with the areas in which they live across the boundaries of time. It was also created as a way to bridge the gap between our lives online and offline.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Where are these photos sourced from?

    The photos for this prototype were sourced from Somerville Archives and Tufts University Libraries' Digital Archives. Future plans include expanding to additional digital photo archives online.

    Who do I contact with questions or feedback?

    Reach out to me via twitter @lolodunnzo or by e-mail at laurenelizabeth.dunn@gmail.com

    Can I upload my own photos to contribute to the project?

    neighborhood.pictures does not currently support upload from individual users, but hopes to include this feature as the project continues to develop and grow.
